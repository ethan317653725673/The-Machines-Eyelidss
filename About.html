About

The Machines Eyelids is multimedia narrative by Ethan Clark.

It uses two forms of media combined to create one immersive experience for the reader / listener.

The chapters are to be read alongside the album, following the same order. However, you can experience the story however you like; you may want to listen to album first and then experience the story, or the other way around.

I hope for you to enjoy the ambient setting of this world, and for you to lose yourself in the dystopian atmosphere of it all.

Best experienced with headphones.
